Hey there, I'm Jenner.

I write the kind of romcoms that make you blush on public transit and laugh loud enough to get dirty looks. My stories are packed with unfiltered heroines who say exactly what they mean, emotionally constipated heroes who need a lot of unpacking, and enough chemistry to set off every smoke detector in the building.

The Writing Journey

Writing wasn't always the plan. But once I picked up a pen (okay, opened a laptop), the stories wouldn't stop coming. I write romcoms because I believe in the power of love stories that make you feel something real ‚Äî the butterflies, the frustration, the ugly-cry moments, and the happily ever afters that feel earned.

Every book I write starts with a question: what if two people who are absolutely wrong for each other are actually exactly right? Then I throw in some chaos, a lot of banter, and just enough heat to keep things interesting.

If you're here, you're my kind of reader. Welcome to the chaos.
